Frequently Asked Questions

Are your adult diapers medical-grade?

Yes. Our adult diapers are manufactured under ISO 13485 (Medical Devices Quality Management System) certification and are CE marked as Class I medical devices for the European market.

What absorbency levels do your adult diapers offer?

We offer three absorbency levels: Moderate (800ml), Heavy (1500ml), and Super (2500ml+). Each level is designed for different usage scenarios 鈥?daytime light incontinence, overnight protection, and heavy-duty care.

Do you produce both tape-on briefs and pull-up style adult diapers?

Yes, we manufacture both styles. Tape-on briefs are ideal for bedridden patients and heavy incontinence. Pull-up pants are designed for active seniors who maintain some mobility.

What sizes are available for adult diapers?

We produce sizes S through XXL, covering waist ranges from 50cm to 160cm. Each size is anatomically designed for optimal fit and leak protection.

Can you customize the absorbency or features?

Yes. As an OEM/ODM manufacturer, we can adjust the SAP-to-fluff ratio for different absorbency targets, add wetness indicators, customize the backsheet material, and modify cuff designs to meet your market requirements.

What is your experience with elderly care facility supply?

We have extensive experience supplying nursing homes, hospitals, and elderly care chains across multiple markets. We offer bulk packaging optimized for institutional use and can provide staff training materials upon request.
