Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for OEM sanitary pads?

Our standard MOQ is 50,000 pieces per SKU, which is approximately one 20ft container. We can discuss smaller trial orders for new partnerships.

Do you offer private label packaging for sanitary pads?

Yes, we provide full private label and OEM packaging services. You can customize the packaging design, logo, color scheme, and even the individual wrapper design.

What certifications do your sanitary pads hold?

Our sanitary pads are manufactured under ISO 9001 and ISO 13485 certified quality management systems. They are CE marked for EU markets and comply with FDA requirements.

What is the difference between cotton-soft and probiotic sanitary pads?

Cotton-soft pads feature a premium natural cotton top layer for maximum comfort and breathability. Probiotic pads add lactobacillus probiotics to the surface layer, which helps maintain healthy vaginal pH and provides antibacterial protection.

Can I order free samples before placing a bulk order?

Absolutely. We offer free samples to qualified buyers so you can evaluate product quality, absorbency, and comfort before committing to a production order. Shipping costs are covered by the buyer.

How long does OEM production and shipping take?

After sample approval, production typically takes 25-35 days depending on order volume and packaging complexity. Shipping from Qingdao port takes 15-30 days to Europe, 12-20 days to the US West Coast.
