In-House Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes rigorous laboratory testing. These essential tests ensure our sanitary pads meet the highest standards of safety, comfort, and performance.

Rewet Test

Measures liquid strike-back to ensure dry surface comfort, a critical factor for user confidence.

Side Leakage Test

Tests lateral barrier effectiveness to prevent leakage during movement and heavy flow.

Breathability Test

Evaluates air permeability to maintain skin health and comfort during extended wear.

Microbiology Test

Ensures products meet stringent microbiological safety standards for intimate use.

Absorption Speed Test

Tests rapid fluid absorption capacity, ensuring instant dryness and leak protection.
